Single Engine Piston Cessna Aircraft Information

A subsidiary of Textron, Cessna Aircraft company is well known for their production of piston-powered aircraft, though they also manufacture a wide range of business jets.

Single engine piston aircraft manufactured by Cessna include the Cessna 120, the Cessna 140 series, the Cessna 150 series, the Cessna 152 series, the Cessna 170 series, the Cessna 172, the Cessna 175, the Cessna 177 Cardinal, the Cessna 180 series, the Cessna 182 Skylane series, the Cessna 182 Turbo Skylane series, the {Cessna 185} series, the Cessna 188, the Cessna 190, the Cessna 195 series, the Cessna 205, the Cessna 206 series, the Cessna 207 series, the Cessna Pressurized 210series, the Cessna 210 series, the Cessna Turbo 210 series, the Cessna 260SE conversion, the Cessna 350, the Cessna 400 series, the Cessna AGhusky, the Cessna AGtruck, the Cessna AgWagon, and the Cessna L-19 series.
